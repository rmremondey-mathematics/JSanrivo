## About JSanrivo

JSanrivo is an **independent, author-led mathematical journal** dedicated to publishing original research in mathematics.  
It aims to provide a **fast, transparent, and fully accessible platform** for sharing ideas without the constraints of traditional journals.  

This repository contains:  
- **Journal/VolXX/** — Published PDFs of papers  
- **Notes/** — GitNote-style workspace for drafts, proofs, and LaTeX snippets  
- **Editorial/** — Scope, Ethics, AI Policy  
- **CITATION.cff** — Citation metadata

## Citation

When citing a paper from JSanrivo, please use the following formats:

**Plain text / general reference:**

Dey, E. (2026). Title of the Paper. JSanrivo — Independent Mathematical Journal. https://github.com/rmremondey-mathematics/JSanrivo

**BibTeX entry:**

@misc{dey2026title,
  author = {Emon Dey},
  title = {Title of the Paper},
  year = {2026},
  howpublished = {JSanrivo — Independent Mathematical Journal},
  url = {https://github.com/rmremondey-mathematics/JSanrivo}
}

## Editorial Policies

- **Scope:** Original mathematics research across all fields  
- **Ethics:** All works follow academic integrity; plagiarism is not tolerated  
- **AI Policy:** AI may be used **only for formatting, LaTeX assistance, or presentation**, not for generating original research
